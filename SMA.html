If a player were to try all the possible combinations in the 6/58 PCSO Lotto, would the grand prize place him in a winning situation?

The Philippine Charity Sweepstake Office, better known as PCSO, regularly holds lotteries wherein bettors place around 20 pesos for a ticket and a chance to win money from the huge prize pool. The mechanics of the 6/58 Ultra Lotto specifically states that there are 58 numbers that players can choose from. Among the 58 numbers, the players have to pick 6 lucky numbers which must match with the 6 winning numbers to take home the grand prize of around 50,000,000 pesos.
